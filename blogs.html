2-May-2023: topic

The school has decided to discontinue the bring your own device network which could result in unforseen issues. The school believes it's 1:1 technology policy has been successful so students bringing their own devices is unneeded. Whether this is just the network being removed or not allowing the students to bring their own device is unclear, however should it be the latter it will have dire consequences in all the technology based classes as the school continues to restrict their access.
